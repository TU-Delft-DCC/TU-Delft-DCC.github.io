# 30-60-90 days checklist

This checklist is designed to help you navigate your first 30, 60, and 90 days in your role, providing a roadmap to ensure a smooth transition and successful integration into our team. This checklist should be used as a guide and adapted to your specific needs and context.

::: {.callout-tip}
## Checklist
**30 days**

- [ ] Meet with colleagues
- [ ] Set up weekly catchup meeting with mentor
- [ ] Familiarize with DCC model
- [ ] Familiarize with the university's organisation and infrastructure.
- [ ] Learn about day-to-day work
- [ ] Identify opportunities for training
- [ ] Sign up for training

**60 days**

- [ ] Identify opportunities within your role and DCC 
- [ ] Identify issues or pain points with the role and responsibilities
- [ ] Scope contributions to project
- [ ] Obtain feedback from supervisor on progress
- [ ] Plan for learning new (required) skills

**90 days**

- [ ] Work independently on project
- [ ] Obtain instructor certification for teaching in Carpentries
- [ ] Reflect on your progress with your supervisor and identify areas for further growth and improvement.
- [ ] Provide feedback to the onboarding process and suggest any enhancements or additional resources that would benefit new RSEs
:::
